About Padiyath
Ayurveda

A Legacy of Healing. A Promise of Wellness.

Padiyath Apasmara Chikitsalayam is a distinguished Ayurveda Chikitsalayam with a rich history dating back to the 1830s. Our journey began with Enikutty Kutty Molla, who received the ancient knowledge of “Apasmara ney” from Mamburam Thangal — precious wisdom passed down through generations, shaping our legacy of holistic healing.

Today, Padiyath Ayurveda Hospital stands as the healthcare arm of the company, dedicated to providing comprehensive Ayurvedic healthcare services. We focus on diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation using authentic Ayurvedic principles and therapies.

Our manufacturing division creates herbal remedies, dietary supplements, skincare items, and medicinal formulations using authentic ingredients and stringent quality standards.

The Origin of

Padiyath

The name “Padiyath” holds a special significance. It was acquired when Moideen Haji Paappa purchased the Padiyath Naalukettu house from Hindu Nair brothers near Valavannur Paadam. This historic house became the epicenter for the creation and preservation of ‘Apasmara neyy,’ giving rise to the name “Padiyath Apasmara Neyy.”

Padiyath offers the best Ayurvedic treatments for all age groups.

Legacy and Expertise

With a legacy spanning 195 years, Padiyath Apasmara Gritham and Chikitsa have been mastered by Enikutty Molla and passed down through generations, reaching its zenith with Padiyath Kunjimon Haji, the fifth generation heir. Our commitment to medical preparations spans 40 years, and Padiyath Pharmaceuticals has stood tall for 18 years, embodying our dedication to traditional healing practices.
