Our Journey

Hospital History

From a modest beginning to a full-service hospital, Padiyath Hospital has grown through decades of dedication, community trust, and an unwavering commitment to better health.

  1. 1998

    The Beginning

    Padiyath Hospital was founded as a small clinic with a team of dedicated physicians committed to serving the local community with affordable, quality care.

  2. 2005

    Expansion & Specialization

    The hospital expanded into a multi-specialty facility, introducing cardiology, orthopedics, and pediatrics departments with modern diagnostic capabilities.

  3. 2012

    Technology Upgrade

    Major investments in advanced imaging, minimally invasive surgery, and ICU infrastructure positioned Padiyath as a regional center for complex care.

  4. 2019

    Centers of Excellence

    Launch of dedicated centers for cardiac care, neurosciences, and maternal health, attracting specialist doctors and serving thousands of patients annually.

  5. Today

    A Trusted Healthcare Partner

    With 20+ departments, 150+ beds, and 25+ years of excellence, Padiyath Hospital continues to evolve — combining premium facilities with the warmth of personalized care.
