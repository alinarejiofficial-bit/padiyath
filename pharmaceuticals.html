About

Padiyath Pharmaceuticals (Manufacturing Company)

Established on June 11, 2005, Padiyath Pharmaceuticals is a pharmaceutical company with drug licenses, GMP licenses, excise licenses, and factories and boilers licenses. Our state-of-the-art facilities produce a range of medicines, including the licensed Apasmara Neyy and 18 patented formulations, such as ‘Aristam and Asava,’ kashayam, lehyam, thailam, gritham, churnam, tablets, postpartum medicines, and syrups.

Padiyath Pharmaceuticals aims to provide end-to-end healthcare solutions, from the development and production of pharmaceuticals to the delivery of high-quality medical care. This integrated approach enables Padiyath Pharmaceuticals to contribute significantly to healthcare advancement, patient outcomes, and community health. This division operates state-of-the-art facilities adhering to stringent quality control measures and regulatory standards. It employs cutting-edge technology and highly skilled professionals to ensure the safety, efficacy, and consistency of its products.
